Using the installed skill, completely redesign the product page from the ground up for both desktop and mobile. Do not reuse any part of the current product page design.

Goal

Create a premium, elegant, high-converting product page that feels modern, fashion-focused, and visually refined. Every element should be carefully proportioned, with strong visual hierarchy and excellent use of whitespace.

Desktop Layout

Design the page with the following structure:

Large product image/gallery centered as the primary focal point.
Product description and key product information on the left.
Price, purchase actions, and variant selection on the right.
Ensure all sections are perfectly balanced and proportioned across the viewport.
Reviews Section

Directly below the product information:

Add a beautiful customer reviews section with a premium, trustworthy appearance.
Display 3 featured reviews by default.
Allow users to either:
Swipe horizontally through reviews, or
Click "View All Reviews" to expand the full review list.
The section should showcase social proof without consuming excessive vertical space.
Recommended Products

Below the reviews section:

Add an auto-scrolling, swipeable carousel of recommended products ("You May Also Like").
Use large product images with the product title displayed beneath each image.
Prioritize visual appeal and product discovery.
The carousel should feel smooth, premium, and luxurious.
Mobile Layout

Research and follow the highest-converting product page patterns used by leading fashion and apparel brands.

Choose the mobile layout based on proven ecommerce conversion principles rather than simply adapting the desktop version. Focus on:

Fast product comprehension
Strong purchase visibility
Smooth thumb-friendly interactions
Minimal friction to purchase
Premium visual presentation
Design Direction
Luxury fashion brand aesthetic
Clean and modern
Elegant typography
Generous spacing
Strong visual hierarchy
Premium imagery presentation
Conversion-focused UX
Important
Do not include any accordion sections.
Do not add "Made in Egypt" or similar origin badges.
Do not introduce additional informational sections that are not directly related to conversion.
Keep the experience focused on the product, reviews, and product discovery.

The objective is to create the most visually appealing and highest-converting product page possible while maintaining a premium fashion-brand feel.
