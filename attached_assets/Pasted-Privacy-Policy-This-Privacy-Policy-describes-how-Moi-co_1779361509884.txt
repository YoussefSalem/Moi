Refund Policy

At Moi, customer satisfaction is important to us.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be approved in the following cases:

* Wrong item received
* Damaged product upon arrival
* Order not delivered

To request a refund, customers must contact us within 3 days of receiving the order.

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are not available for:

* Incorrect size selection by the customer
* Minor color differences due to screen display
* Products damaged after use

Refund Process

Once your refund request is reviewed and approved:

* The refund will be processed through the original payment method or bank transfer
* Processing time may take 5–10 business days

Return Policy
