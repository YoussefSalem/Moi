Redesign the review carousel to feel like a premium luxury ecommerce experience, inspired by modern card-based interfaces.

Requirements:

* Display 3 review cards at all times:
    * 1 primary review card in the center.
    * 2 adjacent review cards on the left and right.
* Only approximately 15% of each side card should be visible, acting as a visual cue that more reviews are available.
* The visible portion of the side cards should not display review text or content. Only the card edge/background should be visible.
* The center card should be the visual focus and appear slightly larger than the side cards.
* Keep the scale difference subtle and elegant:
    * Center card ≈ 95% scale.
    * Side cards ≈ 85% scale.
* As the user swipes, cards should smoothly transition between side and center positions.
* The card approaching the center should gradually scale up.
* The card leaving the center should smoothly scale down into a side position.
* The scaling and positioning must be tied directly to swipe progress for a fluid, premium feel.
* Review content should animate independently from the card movement:
    * As a card moves into the center, its text content should smoothly fade and slide into view.
    * As a card leaves the center, its text content should smoothly fade out.
    * Side cards should not show visible review text.
* Add smooth momentum, easing, and depth so the interaction feels natural and high-end.
* Consider subtle opacity and depth differences between center and side cards to reinforce focus.
* Avoid dramatic scaling, exaggerated motion, or flashy effects.
* No layout shifts, jittering, flickering, snapping, scroll locking, or performance issues.
* Maintain perfect touch responsiveness on mobile devices.
* Support mouse dragging and touch gestures.
* Maintain 60fps performance.
* Preserve all existing review functionality and data.

The final experience should feel like a luxury product showcase carousel: one prominent review in the center, subtle previews of neighboring reviews on each side, and beautifully animated content that appears only when a review becomes the active center card.
